About CAREN GmbH Industrial Machinery

Innovative Consulting Solutions for the Global Food Industry Since 1990

Introduction

CAREN GmbH was established in Germany and with over 35 years of expertise, is a trusted recommender & engineering consultant for food processing machinery and equipment. We proudly serve over 800 domestic clients and provide engineering solutions to more than 68 countries worldwide.

Our Strengths

  • 300 professional engineering consultants
  • A fully equipped 35,000 sqm consulting facility
  • Certified to CE and ISO standards
  • Advanced solution design using state-of-the-art technology
